\(\Rightarrow\) Rolling process (Video 1), (Video 2)
Angle of contact Angle between the tangent to the roll surface and the tangent to the material at the point of contact \(\theta_c=\sqrt{\frac{\Delta H}{R}}\)
Length of contact Length of the arc of material that is in contact with the roll surface \(L_c=R\theta_c=\sqrt{\Delta HR}\)
Condition of self entering The material must be able to enter the roll gap by itself without external force \(\mu >\sqrt{\frac{\Delta H}{R}}\)
Maximum possible reduction per pass The maximum amount by which the thickness of the material can be reduced in a single pass through the rolls \(\Delta H=\mu^2 R\)
Average flow stress The average force required to cause deformation in the material \(\sigma_{avg}=\frac{K \epsilon^n}{1+n}\)
\(\Rightarrow\) Clearance in Punching/Blanking Empirical formula for clearance in punching or blanking operations. \(C = 0.0032t\sqrt{\tau}\)
\(\Rightarrow\) Compulsory approach Formula to calculate the compulsory approach in a punching or blanking operation. \(AP_c = \frac{1}{2}\left(D - \sqrt{D^2 - w^2}\right)\)
\(\Rightarrow\) Time per cut Calculation of time required for each cut in a punching or blanking operation. Time per cut \( = \frac{L_{\text{total}}}{fZN}\)
\(\Rightarrow\) Degree of drawing (D) Calculation of the degree of drawing in a metal forming process. \(D = \frac{A_0 - A_f}{A_0}\)

\(r = \frac{{t_0}}{{t_c}} = \frac{{\sin{\phi}}}{{\cos{\phi-\alpha}}}\)
